INTRODUCTION

ONLINE FREE TUTORIAL IS AN EDUCATIONAL WEBSITE WHERE WE CAN COMPLETE OUR COURSE AND GET CERTIFICATE THROUGH ONLINE AT FREE OF COST.
IT IS HELPFUL FOR THE LEARNERS TO COMPLETE THE COURSE WITH IN THE SHORT PERIOD OF TIME.
THIS WEBSITE IS ALSO USEFUL FOR THE INSTRUCTOR TO SHARE THEIR KNOWLEDGE.
IT HAS QUIZ SESSION SO THAT LEARNERS CAN DEVELOP THEIR KNOWLEDGE AND EVALUATE THEMSELVES.

LEARNER MODULE WITH LOGIN AND SIGN UP

IN THIS MODULE THE LEARNER NEED TO SIGN IN WITH VALID USERNAME AND PASSWORD.
IF THE LEARNER DON’T HAVE THE ACCOUNT, LEARNER NEED TO SIGN UP WITH FIRST NAME, SUR NAME , EMAIL ID AND PASSWORD ,THEN MAIL WILL BE SENT TO YOUR MAIL ID REGADING ONLINE FREE TUTORIAL

INSTRUCTOR MODULE WITH LOGIN AND SIGN UP

IN THIS MODULE THE INSTRUCTOR NEED TO SIGN IN WITH VALID INSTRUCTOR ID AND PASSWORD.
INSTRUCTOR ID SHOULD BE IN THE COMBINATION OF ALPHA NUMERICAL.
IF THE INSTRUCTOR DON’T HAVE THE ACCOUNT , INSTRUCTOR NEED TO SIGN UP WITH FIRST NAME, EMAIL ID, HANDLING COURSE(WHICH COURSE GOING TO BE HANDLED) AND PASSWORD, THEN MAIL WILL BE SENT TO YOUR MAIL ID REGARDING ONLINE FREE TUTORIAL.

INSTRUCTOR – COURSE UPLOAD

IN THIS MODULE INSTRUCTOR WILL UPLOAD THE COURSE MATERIAL FOR THE COURSES LIKE PERSONAL SKILL DEVELOPMENT,C,C++,JAVA,..WHAT HE/SHE IS HANDLING.
THE COURSE MATERIALS ARE AVAILABLE IN THIS WEBSITE IN THE FORM OF TEXT FORMAT , PDF FORMAT , VIDEO FORMAT AND E-BOOK.

LEARNER – COURSE COMPLETION

THIS MODULE IS HELPFUL FOR THE LEARNERS TO COMLETE THE COURSE.
LEARNER NEED TO ATTEND THE LECTURE HOUR OF WHAT ARE THE MATERIALS UPLOADED BY THE INSTRUCTOR.
LEARNERS NEED TO DOWNLOAD THE COURSE MATERIALS UPLOADED BY THE INSTRUCTOR.
ONCE ALL THE SESSION IS COMPLETED THE LEARNER’S COURSE IS COMPLETED.

LEARNER QUIZ AND CERTIFICATION

THIS MODULE IS THE FINAL MODULE OF ONLINE FEE TUTORIAL.
AFTER THE COMPLETION OF COURSE LEARNER NEED TO ATTEND THE QUIZ SESSION.
IT CONSISTS OF THIRTY MULTIPLE CHOICE QUESTION.
AFTER COMPLETION OF QUIZ SESSION , IT WILL GENERATE YOU CERTIFICATE , YOU CAN DOWNLOAD AS JPG OR PDF FORMAT.
